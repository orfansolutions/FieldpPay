# PaY Flow: User Manual & Operational Guide

## 1. Introduction to PaY Flow

**PaY Flow** is a professional, high-performance expense management and payroll platform designed to transform how organisations handle their financial workflows. By moving away from manual, paper-based systems, PaY Flow provides a fully digitised environment that streamlines the entire payment lifecycle—from initial requisition to final disbursement.

### Core Purpose
The primary goal of PaY Flow is to **eliminate paperwork** and reduce administrative friction. It achieves this by:
*   **Digitising the Payment Process**: No more physical forms. Every request is tracked, timestamped, and moved through a secure digital approval chain.
*   **Project Cost Management**: Link every cent spent to specific projects and phases, ensuring you never exceed your budget.
*   **Live Expense Tracking**: Real-time dashboards provide instant visibility into your organisation's financial health, VAT liabilities, and upcoming payment obligations.
*   **SARS-Compliant Payroll**: A built-in payroll engine that handles tax brackets, UIF, SDL, and net pay calculations automatically.

## 2. Getting Started: Setting Up Your Organisation

### 2.1 Creating an Account
1.  **Sign Up**: Visit the login page and select "Create Account".
2.  **Initial Setup**: Upon your first login, you will be prompted to set up your **Organisation Profile**.
3.  **Financial Year**: Define your financial year start and end dates. This is critical for accurate reporting and budget tracking.

### 2.2 Building the Organisational Structure
Navigate to **Settings > Organisation Settings** to build your foundation:
*   **Departments**: Create departments (e.g., Marketing, Operations, IT). These act as the primary cost-allocation units.
*   **Cost Centres**: Define specific cost centres for more granular tracking. You can import these via CSV template to save time.
*   **Payment Cycles**: Set up how your organisation pays (Weekly, Bi-weekly, or Monthly). PaY Flow will automatically calculate payment dates based on your financial year and public holidays.

## 3. User Roles and Permissions

PaY Flow uses a robust Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system to ensure security and data integrity:

| Role | Permissions Overview |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Super User** | Full access to all settings, user management, and data clearing. Usually reserved for the system administrator. |
| **CEO / CFO** | High-level oversight. Can approve large requisitions and view all financial reports and payroll data. |
| **Financial Manager** | Manages the day-to-day financial operations, processes payroll, and handles VAT reporting. |
| **Manager** | Can manage their own department, approve requisitions within their budget, and view project reports. |
| **Requester** | The standard user role. Can create requisitions, manage contacts, and view their own submitted requests. |

## 4. Operational Workflows

### 4.1 Digitised Requisitions
The heart of PaY Flow is the requisition system:
1.  **Create**: A Requester fills out a digital form, attaching an invoice and linking it to a **Project** and **Department**.
2.  **Approval Flow**: The request automatically moves to the relevant Manager. If it exceeds certain thresholds, it escalates to the CFO.
3.  **Editing**: Approvers (Managers, CFOs, etc.) can edit a requisition before approving it to correct errors, ensuring data accuracy without requiring the user to resubmit.
4.  **Live Status**: Users can track their request status (Pending, Approved, Paid, or Rejected) in real-time.

### 4.2 Project Management
*   **Create Projects**: Define project names, total budgets, and specific phases.
*   **Live Tracking**: As requisitions are approved, the project dashboard updates instantly, showing "Actual vs. Budget" expenditure.
*   **AI Insights**: Use the "Analyse with AI" button on the dashboard to get a professional assessment of project spending risks.

### 4.3 Payroll Management
1.  **Employee Records**: Add employees with their gross salary, bank details, and tax information.
2.  **Deductions**: Link recurring deductions (Medical Aid, Pension) to employee profiles.
3.  **Processing**: Each month, the Financial Manager "Processes Monthly Payroll". The system calculates PAYE, UIF, and SDL based on current SARS brackets.
4.  **Payslips**: Generate professional PDF payslips for all employees with a single click.

## 5. Advanced Features

### 5.1 PFAI: Your AI Assistant
Located at the bottom right of your screen, **PFAI** is powered by Gemini. You can ask it to:
*   "Draft an invitation email for a new Financial Manager."
*   "Analyse my project expenditure for any risks."
*   "Explain how the VAT calculation for this month was reached."

### 5.2 Progressive Web App (PWA)
You can install PaY Flow as a native app on your phone or desktop:
1.  Look for the **"Download App"** button in the Sidebar or Dashboard.
2.  Follow the browser prompt to "Install".
3.  The app will now appear on your home screen/desktop and work even with poor internet connectivity.

## 6. Support and Maintenance
*   **Settings**: Use the Settings page to update your personal profile, change your password, or update the organisation's VAT rate.
*   **Refresh**: If you notice data is stale, use the "Refresh App" button in the Settings header to force a sync with the cloud database.
